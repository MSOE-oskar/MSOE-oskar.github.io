MSOE Mindset Game


Team design project for my Human-Centered Design class in Fall 2023.
Collaborated with a team of 5 to design a game over a sprint of 3 weeks.
I conducted interviews, and helped to create a user persona, journey map, wireframes, and a prototype.

The game was designed to teach students the different components of the MSOE mindset through participation in campus activities and a leaderboard system.

Project Analysis


This project was my first time doing a group project in person. I was homeschooled prior coming to MSOE, and attended Gateway Technical College entirely online, so any group project I did there was also online. I was a little worried, however I had a great team and we were able to successfully complete the project.

I am proud of the work I did on the project, which consisted of creating the home and leaderboard page in the prototype, conducting an interview, and helping to create the persona and journey map. I am also happy with my communication, I think all members were good at communicating what needed to be done and what they needed help with.

One thing I think could be improved in the future is my communication. Although I was happy with it during the project, I still feel that I could have done better. In the future I plan to be more proactive in reaching out to my teammates when I need help or have questions, and make sure everyone is on the same page and knows what to do.

Overall, I think this project was really good experience, as I learned a lot about UX process and working on a team. I look forward to working with others in the future.
