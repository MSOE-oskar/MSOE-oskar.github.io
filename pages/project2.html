MSOE Mindset Game


Team design project for my Human-Centered Design class in Fall 2023.
Collaborated with a team of 5 to design a game over a sprint of 3 weeks.
Conducted interviews, created a user persona, journey map, wireframes, and a prototype.

The game was designed to teach students the different components of the MSOE mindset through participation in campus activities and a leaderboard system.
